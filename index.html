Good to know

Questions, answered

Do I need an appointment?

No. Walk-ins are always welcome at Goodfellas. If you want a specific barber or a set time, you can book a chair online and skip the wait.

What does a cut cost?

Goodfellas keeps neighborhood pricing, the kind reviewers call reasonable for the quality. For exact current prices on cuts, fades, beards and shaves, give the shop a quick call at (208) 501-6359.

Do you do beards and straight razor shaves?

Yes. Beard trims, lineups and traditional hot towel straight razor shaves are some of the things Goodfellas is known for, alongside razor fades and hair designs.

Do you cut kids' and teens' hair?

Absolutely. Goodfellas is a family shop. Plenty of regulars bring their kids and teens, and the barbers take their time to make sure they leave looking sharp.

Where are you and what are your hours?

We are at 5663 E Franklin Rd, Nampa, ID 83687. Hours are Monday 9 to 2, Tuesday to Friday 9 to 6, Saturday 9 to 4, and closed Sunday.
